Frequently Asked Questions

Gross salary is your total pay before deductions. Net (take-home) pay is after income tax, CPP/EI (Canada) or FICA/Medicare (USA). In Canada at $85,000 in Ontario, net pay is typically 68–72% of gross. Use our Income Tax Calculator for a detailed breakdown.
Most provinces require 1.5× for hours over 44/week. Alberta: 1.5× over 8 hrs/day or 44 hrs/week. BC: 1.5× over 8 hrs/day, 2× over 12 hrs/day. USA: 1.5× for all hours over 40/week under FLSA.
