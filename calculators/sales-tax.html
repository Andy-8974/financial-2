Canadian Tax Structure

HST Provinces

Ontario (13%), Nova Scotia (15%), NB (15%), PEI (15%), NL (15%). One combined rate — no separate PST.

GST+PST Provinces

BC (5+7=12%), Manitoba (5+7=12%), Saskatchewan (5+6=11%). Tax is collected separately.

GST Only

Alberta, Yukon, NWT, Nunavut — 5% federal GST only. Alberta has no provincial sales tax, making it the lowest-tax province for purchases.

Quebec (QST)

Quebec has its own sales tax: QST at 9.975%. Combined with 5% GST = 14.975% effective rate. QST is calculated on the price including GST.
